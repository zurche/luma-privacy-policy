Privacy Policy

Luma Screen Saver

Last updated: January 2026

Luma Screen Saver (“the App”) is a screensaver and ambient display application for Android phones, tablets, and Android TV devices. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the App handles data.

1. Data Collection

Luma Screen Saver does not collect, store, or share personal data.

  • No user accounts
  • No names, emails, or identifiers
  • No location tracking
  • No contacts, photos, camera, or microphone access
  • No analytics or tracking services

All functionality runs locally on the device.

2. Device Permissions

The App may request limited system permissions required for screensaver functionality (such as accessing system time or displaying on screen). These permissions are used only to provide core features.

3. Payments

Optional premium features may be available via Google Play Billing. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google.

The App does not receive or store payment information. Google’s Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Advertising

Luma Screen Saver does not display ads and does not use advertising SDKs.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. It does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Data Sharing

Since no personal data is collected, no data is shared with third parties.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
